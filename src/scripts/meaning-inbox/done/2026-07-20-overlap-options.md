The pheromones and keywords we leave locally for ourselves overlap after a bit
— family, photos, friends, business, whatever. If a combination makes sense
from a meta point of view — say you have family AND photos — maybe you want a
photo website or a photo gallery. So the AI determines that, or asks: "you've
got this combination of stuff here — do you want to build this?" — or at least
provides some options of what you could do here and starts to build out a hive
of where it could go from here.

The options are tiles, basically. You discard the ones you don't want, the
ones you're not sure about you just leave there, or you go ahead with some of
the possibilities — like introducing a website.

So it's the overlapping of pheromones that gets interpreted by the AI — and
hopefully a simple AI too, because this is just a categorical question, it's
not much to do.
